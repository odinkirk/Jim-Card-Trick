Jim's Card Trick

Do you have your own deck of cards?

Time to Shuffle

Shuffle your deck of cards. Shuffle them any way you like, just be sure they're randomized. Click the button to proceed when you're ready.

Cut the Deck

Cut the deck roughly in half. It does not have to be perfect, but it should be close. Keep the two halves separate for now. Click the button to move on to the next step.

Count How Many

Take the top half of the deck and keep it remaining face down. Count the number of cards in this half and make a note of the number. Click the button when youre ready to move on to the next step.

Add Them Up

Add the digits in the answer together arriving at a smaller number. For example: If you were a card ninja and cut the deck perfectly, arriving at 26 cards in the top half, we would add 2 plus 6 and get 8 as your magic number. When you've added up your digits and found your magic number, click the button to proceed.

Your Card Revealed

From here forward, avoid rearranging the cards. Turn top half of the deck face up and count throught the cards to the magic number from the last step. This is your magic card, so keep it in mind. When you've got it memorized, click the button.

Putting it all together

Being sure to keep the cards in the order they were in at the start of the last step, place the top half of the deck back (face down) on top of the rest of the deck. Then, click the button below.

And your card is...
